# General Questions

## What is CelestialDocs?

CelestialDocs is a modern documentation template built with Astro and Shadcn UI.

## Is it free to use?

Yes, CelestialDocs is open source and free to use.

## Can I customize it?

Absolutely! CelestialDocs is fully customizable to match your brand.
